Privacy Policy for Appminton

Last updated: 12 February 2026

Appminton respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the app and provide session management features.

Account Information

  • Name or nickname
  • Email address or phone number (used for login)

Session & Group Information

  • Groups you join or create
  • Sessions you create or participate in
  • Attendance records
  • Scores and game results

Cost & Payment-Related Information

  • Court booking cost
  • Shuttle usage and cost allocation
  • Payment responsibilities between players (amounts only)
  • Payment status (e.g. pending or paid)

Technical Information

  • Device type
  • App version
  • Crash and error logs

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Contacts from your phone
  • Precise location or background tracking
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Personal files or photos

3. Payments

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers. Appminton does not store your bank details, card numbers, or e-wallet credentials. We only receive payment confirmation and transaction references.

4. Serve Credit Transactions

We collect and store information related to Serve credit purchases, including transaction references and credit balances, for the purpose of enabling session creation and maintaining account records.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage badminton sessions
  • Display attendance, scores, and session history
  • Calculate and show cost breakdowns
  • Display payment responsibilities
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Provide customer support

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is shared only with other players within the same session or group, and with trusted service providers required to operate the app.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request account deletion at any time.

8. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Children’s Privacy

Appminton is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
